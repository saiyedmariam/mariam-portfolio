AboutMe

B. DESIGN 09/01/1999

An interior designer,born in KSA and raised in different countries. Having experienced two cultures which are similar yet different to each other in many ways, I believe I have the ability to bring them together in a perfect harmony. My journey through different societies while growing up has greatly inspired my style of designing and creativity. This world is getting smaller by the day. Meaning many cultures are coming together and creating new possibilities. That’s the kind of change that I wish to bring through my designs and creations
